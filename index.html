Discover Tyrrell County

Our county is a hidden gem with a rich history, scenic landscapes, and a tight-knit community. From the historic town of Columbia to the tranquil shores of the sound, Tyrrell County offers a unique blend of small-town warmth and outdoor adventures.

Whether you're interested in birdwatching at the Pocosin Lakes National Wildlife Refuge, immersing yourself in local history at Somerset Place, or simply enjoying the peaceful surroundings, Tyrrell County has something for everyone.
